FAQ

If you have questions, we have the answers

Answers to the most frequently asked questions about booking stadiums, payment, and using Fit Sport.

How do I book a stadium?

You can book a stadium by browsing available playgrounds, selecting your preferred time slot, and completing the booking process through our platform.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple payment methods including Fawry, Paymob, and cash on delivery for your convenience.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can cancel your booking up to 2 hours before the scheduled time. Cancellation policies may vary by venue.

How do I join a team or match?

Browse available matches in your area, check the skill level requirements, and join teams looking for players in your preferred sport.
